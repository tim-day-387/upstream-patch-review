LU-0000 tests: add test for parity mirror operations

Add a test to verify that O_APPEND and truncate are
properly blocked on parity mirrors while being allowed
on data mirrors.

The test creates an EC file with a parity mirror and
verifies:
1. O_APPEND is blocked when setting designated mirror
   on a parity mirror
2. Truncate is blocked on a designated parity mirror
3. O_APPEND is allowed on a designated data mirror
4. Truncate is allowed on a designated data mirror
