LU-18222 nodemap: add lqa_quota_ops rbac role

The purpose of the new lqa_quota_ops rbac role is to handle
lqa-related quota commands. When not set, clients cannot set lqa
specific quotas.

sanity-sec test_64c is updated to exercise the new lqa_quota_ops rbac
role.
