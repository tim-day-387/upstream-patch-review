LU-18181 tests: replace "$LCTL pool_new" with pool_add()

This patch replaces "$LCTL pool_new" with pool_add()
in t32_test() to make sure the OST pool is finally
created. The patch also replaces "$LCTL pool_add" with
pool_add_targets() to add OST targets into the pool.
