LU-12668 ec: Update resync and verify to block granularity

Instead of writing and verifying the entire parity stripes
compute what regions of the parity stripes are actually protecting
data and what is not and only write/verify those blocks.

This greatly reduces the storage required for small files.

Example: --ec 4+2 and stripe_size 4M and a small file containing 6 bytes.
Instead of this requiring 2 * 4MB of parity storage
we will now only consume 2 fs blocks on the OST.
