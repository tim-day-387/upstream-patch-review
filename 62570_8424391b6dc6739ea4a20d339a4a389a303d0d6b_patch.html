LU-19545 lod: add tight binding between parity and data

This patch implements tight binding between parity
mirrors and their corresponding data mirrors in
Lustre's erasure coding (EC) implementation.

The wire protocol structure lov_comp_md_entry_v1 is
modified to store the mirror ID of the data mirror in
corresponding parity components. The lcme_timestamp
field (64-bit) is split into lcme_data_id (16-bit),
lcme_timestamp_sec_high (16-bit), and
lcme_timestamp_sec_low (32-bit) to accommodate this.

Helper functions lu_monter_time_16_32() and
lu_demonter_time_16_32() are added for timestamp
conversion.
