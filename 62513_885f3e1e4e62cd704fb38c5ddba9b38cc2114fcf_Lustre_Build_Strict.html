PATCH:
LU-19591 mgs: support multiple MGS's

Add support to start multiple MGS's:
. The starter MGS(MGS on MDT0 or separate MGS) is used to bring
  MDTs/OSTs up, and it also serves clients in the old way.
. User can start MGS on other MDTs by adding mount option "mgs",
  note this should be done on a Raft node. And these MGS's can
  serve clients.
. Since each MDS can have only one MGS, if there are several MDTs
  on an MDS, and they are all mounted with "-o mgs", the MGS is
  started on the first MDT.
. don't start MGS on Raft nodes where the MGS0 may be running,
  e.g. MGS on MDT0 (located on MDS0), and may be failed over to MDS1,
  do not start MGS on other MDTs on both MDS0 and MDS1, otherwise
  it may cause brain split or MDT0 failover will fail.
. MGS other than MGS0 will erase all target configs upon start.
. MGS on Raft node will generate the config llog of itself (the
  combined MDT), and the other MDT/OST config llogs after handshakes
  with them.

Add sanity-raft.sh test_2 to verify this feature.
