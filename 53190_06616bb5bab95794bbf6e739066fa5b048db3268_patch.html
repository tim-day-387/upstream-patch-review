LU-15372 mdd: Add projid in Changelog

This patch adds projid support into Changelog.
Projid would be printed under section "projid="
Where projid is not applicable it will come
out as 0.

"projid= will be followed by flg=. Where,
flag is file attribute flag (eg LUSTRE_PROJINHERIT_FL)
